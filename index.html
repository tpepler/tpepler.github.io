Hi, I'm Theo, and welcome to my home page. I am a research associate at the University of Glasgow, applied statistician, and all-round data analyst. My work is funded by the Scottish Government's Centre of Expertise on Animal Disease Outbreaks (EPIC).

I am passionate about statistical computing, free software and machine learning, amongst other things. My research interests are in multivariate statistics (in particular principal component analysis), the bootstrap and other resampling methods, nonparametric statistics, biostatistics, and statistical epidemiology.
