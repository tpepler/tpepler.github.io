Hi, I'm Theo, and welcome to my home page. I work as a biostatistician at the Robertson Centre for Biostatistics, which is part of the Glasgow Clinical Trials Unit (GCTU) at the University of Glasgow.

I am passionate about statistical computing, free software and open science. My research interests are in biostatistics, clinical trials, multivariate statistics (principal component analysis), the bootstrap and other resampling methods, nonparametric statistics, and simulation modelling.
