Napoletana pasta sauce (fresh tomatoes)

Serves 4 people

This is a versatile tomato pasta sauce, made with fresh tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 500g unpeeled red tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • olive oil
  • fresh basil

    • Preparation

    1. Add the tomatoes to boiling water and boil until the skins start to crack.
    2. Remove the tomatoes from the water and peel the skins.
    3. Chop the garlic clove and pan-fry in olive oil for a couple of seconds. Avoid frying until the garlic turns brown, as this will create a bitter taste.
    4. Add the tomatoes, garlic and some chopped basil to a saucepan and cook until the tomatoes are soft and disintegrating.
    5. If preferred, blend the sauce until fine. Serve hot over pasta of your choice.
