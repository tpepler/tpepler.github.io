Soup -- STILL TO TRY OUT!

Serves ?? people

This is an easy and quick to prepare soup, to make with whatever vegetables you have at hand.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • vegetables
  • small pasta / barley (optional)
  • 1 block of vegetable/chicken/beef stock
  • 1 litre hot water
  • fresh herbs
  • salad leaves (e.g. rocket)

Preparation

  1. Chop the vegetables in small pieces.
  2. Saute the onion in a soup pot.
  3. Dissolve the stock in the hot water. Add the stock, vegetables and pasta/barley to the soup pot and cook for about 20 minutes.
  4. Add the fresh herbs and salad leaves last.
