Bean and tuna salad

Serves 2-3 people

This quick salad serves two to three people, either as a small main meal, or a side. Goes well with oatcakes or bread!

Ingredients

  • 1 can mixed beans
  • 1 can tuna (in water)
  • small onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil

Preparation

  1. Drain the cans of tuna and beans. Do not drain the beans completely---it is good to leave some of the sauce for the salad.
  2. Chop the onion finely and add a small amount to the salad according to taste.
  3. Add salt, pepper and olive oil to taste.
