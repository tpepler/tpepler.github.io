Scottish oatcakes

Makes about 14 oatcakes

These gluten-free oatcakes make for a tasty snack. Recipe adapted from the one posted here.

Ingredients

  • 225g oats
  • 60g wholewheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 60g unsalted butter (if using salted butter, leave out the 1/2 tsp salt)
  • ~80ml hot water (depending on the oats used)

    • Preparation

    1. Pre-heat the oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
    2. Mix together the oats, flour, salt, sugar and bicarbonate of soda.
    3. Add the butter and rub together until everything is mixed and has the consistency of large bread crumbs.
    4. Add the water (from a recently boiled kettle) bit by bit and combine until you have a somewhat thick dough. The amount of water varies, depending on the oats.
    5. Roll out the dough to about 4-5mm in thickness. Cut out the oatcakes in shapes you prefer.
    6. Place the oatcakes on a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake for approximately 25 minutes or until slightly golden brown.
