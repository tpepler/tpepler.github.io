Napoletana pasta sauce (canned tomatoes)

Serves 10-12 people

This is a versatile tomato pasta sauce, made with canned tomatoes. It can serve as a base for many other tomato based pasta sauces.

Ingredients

  • 8 cans whole peeled plum tomatoes
  • 2 medium sized onions, chopped
  • 2 small carrots, chopped
  • 3 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • sunflower oil
  • herbs (basil, rosemary)

    • Preparation

    1. Saute the chopped onions and carrots in some sunflower oil.
    2. Add the tomatoes, sugar, salt and herbs, and blend with a stick blender until smooth.
    3. Add some more sunflower oil (to your taste), and let it simmer for 2 hours on low heat.
    4. The sauce can be frozen for future use.
