Gnocchi (potato dumplings)

Serves 4 - 6 people

These potato dumplings can also be frozen, ready for a quick meal. Serve with a pasta sauce of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1kg unpeeled potatoes (any kind that makes good mash potatoes)
  • 300g flour
  • 1 egg

    • Preparation

    1. Boil the unpeeled potatoes for 25 - 30 minutes until cooked through.

    2. Drain the potatoes and peel the skins.

    3. Add the egg and mash the potatoes.

    4. Add the flour and mix well to form the potato dough.

    5. Roll out the dough in sausage shapes. Cut the dough sausages in small wheels to form the gnocchi portions. Roll the portions in small balls and flatten the individual dumplings slightly with a fork.

    6. To cook, add the required portion of dumplings to boiling water. After a couple of minutes, the dumplings will rise and float in the water. Cook for a further minute before draining the water and serving the dumplings.
